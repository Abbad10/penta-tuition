Course Curriculum

Module 1

Introduction to Artificial Intelligence for Kids

In this module, children will be introduced to the world of Artificial Intelligence. They will explore how AI powers the world around them, from smart assistants to self-driving cars. Through interactive activities, they'll understand how AI learns and makes decisions.

  • What is Artificial Intelligence? Understanding Smart Machines
  • How AI is Used in Everyday Life
  • Understanding Data and How AI Learns
  • Exploring AI through Fun Games & Activities
  • Introduction to Image & Text Recognition
  • Creating an AI-powered Storytelling Project

Hands-on AI Experiments

Children will start working on basic AI experiments using visual tools. They’ll explore how AI can recognize patterns, understand commands, and process images and sounds.

  • Building an AI-powered Chatbot
  • Training AI to Recognize Handwriting
  • Understanding Voice Recognition with AI
  • Creating a Fun AI Drawing Game
  • Exploring How AI Predicts Outcomes
  • Making AI Guess Emotions from Images

Module 2

Machine Learning Basics for Kids

In this module, kids will take their AI knowledge further by learning about Machine Learning. They will explore how AI improves over time by learning from data. They'll also start working on small ML-based projects.

  • What is Machine Learning? AI That Learns!
  • Understanding Datasets & How Machines Learn
  • Training a Simple AI Model
  • Using AI to Recognize Shapes & Patterns
  • Exploring Facial Recognition with AI
  • Building an AI-powered Sorting Game

Module 3

Advanced AI & Neural Networks

This module introduces kids to more advanced AI concepts, including neural networks and deep learning. They’ll explore how AI can recognize and process complex information.

  • Introduction to Neural Networks
  • How AI Recognizes Objects & Faces
  • Using AI to Detect Emotions
  • Building an AI Voice Assistant
  • Exploring AI in Robotics & Automation
  • Final AI Project: Creating an AI-driven Game
